===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples about riches in the world and rewards in heaven.

  * **in the new birth** - "at the time when all things are made new" or "in the new age"
  * **sit upon twelve thrones, judging** - "be kings and judges over" (See: [[:ms:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
